Flow 1 — Customer Dine-In Full Journey
[GUEST]   Scans QR code on table → sees public menu, photos, ratings (no login needed)
           |
           ↓  (decides to order — prompted to register or log in)
[USER]    Registers or logs in via email or username
           |
           ↓  (optional: reserve in advance)
[USER]    Makes reservation: selects date, time slot, party size, table preference
          Optionally pre-selects menu items (pre-order)
           |
           ↓  EVENT: reservation.confirmed → SMS + email reminder scheduled (24h before)
           |
[USER]    Arrives, scans QR → app recognises table, activates table session
[WAITER]  Marks reservation as SEATED in their app
           |
           ↓  EVENT: reservation.seated → pre-ordered items automatically pushed to kitchen queue
[USER]    Can add more items from phone at any time → Order & Kitchen Service
[WAITER]  Can also add items on behalf of customer (for non-app users)
           |
           ↓  EVENT: order.placed → KitchenHub pushes new order to chef screen
[CHEF]    Sees new order (audio chime + animated card) on KDS or phone
          Marks items: QUEUED → PREPARING → READY
           |
           ↓  When all items READY: EVENT: order.ready → WaiterHub notifies assigned waiter
[WAITER]  Picks up food, brings to table. Marks order SERVED.
           |
           ↓  EVENT: order.served → TableHub pushes "Your order has arrived" to customer app
[USER]    Eats. When done, taps "Request Bill" in app.
           |
          Payment options:
          A) Pay via app (Stripe card or account balance) → invoice marked PAID
          B) Waiter brings physical bill → customer pays cash to cashier
             → Cashier marks paid in POS app → CashierHub notifies → table released
           |
           ↓  EVENT: payment.succeeded → bonus points calculated + added to account
           ↓  EVENT: bonus.earned → in-app notification "You earned X points!"
           |
[USER]    Receives review prompt (15 min after payment)
          Rates: Chef, Waiter, Cleanliness + Comment
           |
           ↓  EVENT: review.submitted → Restaurant Service updates aggregated ratings
Flow 2 — Delivery Order
[USER]    Opens app → Delivery tab → selects items from menu → enters address
          Sees total (food + delivery fee). Checks account balance — must cover 100%.
           |
           ↓  If balance insufficient → prompted to top up via Stripe
[USER]    Confirms order → balance HELD (not deducted yet)
           |
           ↓  EVENT: order.placed (type=DELIVERY) → kitchen queue (same as dine-in)
[CHEF]    Prepares order. Marks all items READY.
           |
           ↓  EVENT: order.ready → Delivery Service creates delivery record
           ↓  Broadcast to all ONLINE couriers via CourierHub (SignalR) + SMS (Twilio)
           |
[COURIER] First courier to tap "Accept" gets assigned (Redis lock prevents double-accept)
           |
           ↓  EVENT: delivery.assigned → customer notified with courier name + phone
[COURIER] Picks up order at restaurant. Adds GPS checkpoint "Picked up"
[COURIER] En-route: adds GPS checkpoints periodically
[COURIER] Arrives at address → taps "Delivered"
           |
[USER]    Receives notification: "Your order has arrived!"
          Taps "Confirm Receipt" in app   (or auto-confirmed after 2 hours)
           |
           ↓  Balance HOLD converted to DEDUCTION → restaurant receives funds
           ↓  EVENT: payment.succeeded → bonus points added
           ↓  EVENT: delivery.confirmed → courier rating prompt shown to customer
[USER]    Rates courier: 1–5 stars + comment (optional)
Flow 3 — Chef Kitchen Display
[CHEF]    Opens KDS on kitchen monitor or phone → authenticates → connects to KitchenHub
          Joins SignalR group: kitchen:{restaurantId}
           |
           ↓  New order arrives (from customer app or waiter):
[KDS]     Audio chime plays + new order card slides in
          Shows: table number, customer name, items + modifiers + notes, allergy flags
           |
[CHEF]    Taps item → marks PREPARING
          When item is done → marks READY
           |
           ↓  When ALL items in order = READY:
[CHEF]    Taps "Call Waiter" → selects waiter assigned to that table
          WaiterHub sends push notification to that waiter: "Order for Table 4 ready"
           |
[WAITER]  Receives notification on phone with table number → picks up food
           |
           ↓  If kitchen printer is configured:
[KDS]     On order.placed → POST to printer webhook endpoint
          Printer outputs: order ID, table, items, modifiers, special notes, timestamp
           |
[CHEF]    Can also mark item UNAVAILABLE (86'd) at any time
          → EVENT: menu.item.unavailable → Restaurant Service hides from public menu in real-time
Flow 4 — Loyalty Bonus System
[DIRECTOR] Configures loyalty program:
           Points rate: 1 point per $1 spent (configurable)
           Bonus catalogue: Hotdog = 50pts, Burger = 120pts, Soda = 30pts, Mayo = 10pts
           |
[USER]    Pays for dine-in or delivery order (any payment method)
           |
           ↓  EVENT: payment.succeeded → Payment Service calculates earned points
          points_earned = floor(total_amount × points_rate)
          Added to user_balances.loyalty_points
           |
           ↓  EVENT: bonus.earned → NotifyHub pushes in-app notification
           |
[USER]    Next visit → opens "My Bonuses" screen
          Sees: current points balance + available rewards
          Selects: "Redeem 50pts for Free Hotdog"
           |
           ↓  Redemption creates a line item on the next invoice:
          { name: "Hotdog (Bonus Reward)", quantity: 1, unitPrice: 0.00, originalPrice: 4.50 }
          loyalty_points deducted by 50
           |
           ↓  EVENT: bonus.redeemed → receipt shows item with $0.00 "(Redeemed with points)"
Flow 5 — Cashier POS
[CASHIER]  Opens dedicated POS app → authenticates with CASHIER role
           Sees: live grid of all tables with statuses (EMPTY / OCCUPIED / BILL_REQUESTED)
           |
[WAITER]  Customer asks for bill → waiter taps "Send to Cashier" in their app
           |
           ↓  CashierHub pushes: "Table 7 bill requested — Total: $87.50"
[CASHIER] Sees Table 7 highlighted on their grid
          Views itemized bill: all orders for that table session
           |
           ↓  Customer pays CASH to waiter → waiter hands to cashier
[CASHIER] Taps "Mark as Paid (Cash)" → enters amount received → calculates change
          Invoice status → PAID. Table status → AVAILABLE.
           |
           ↓  CashierHub pushes to WaiterHub: "Table 7 cleared — ready for next guest"
           ↓  EVENT: payment.cash.recorded → bonus points calculated for customer
           ↓  Receipt email sent automatically via SendGrid
           ↓  Review prompt sent to customer 15 min later
